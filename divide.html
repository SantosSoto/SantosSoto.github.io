Lesson 1: Division

Chapter 1: introduction to Division

in this chapter we will learn how division works 8/2 = 4. division is the process of splitting a number into equal parts or groups.

Let's talk about an example: 10 / 2 = 5. This means that 10 is being divided into 2 equal parts, and each part is 5. another example is 20 / 4 = 5. This means that 20 is being divided into 4 equal parts, and each part is 5.

Notes to remember:

- Division is the opposite of multiplication.
- The number being divided is called the dividend.
- The number you are dividing by is called the divisor.
- The result of a division problem is called the quotient.
dividend / divisor = quotient
