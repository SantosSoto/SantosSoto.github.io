Lesson 1: Multiplication

Chapter 1: introduction to multiplication

in this chapter we will learn how multiplication works 3 * 5. multiplication is the process of combining two numbers to get a larger number.

Let's talk about an example: 6 * 3. This means that 6 is being added 3 times 6 + 6 + 6 this gives us the answer of 18. another example is 4 * 7. This means that 4 is being added 7 times 4 + 4 + 4 + 4 + 4 + 4 + 4 this gives us the answer of 28.

Notes to remember:

- Multiplication is the opposite of division.
- The numbers being multiplied are called factors.
- The result of multiplication is called the product.
- Multiplying by 0 always gives 0.
- Multiplying by 1 gives the same number.
factor1 * factor2 = product
